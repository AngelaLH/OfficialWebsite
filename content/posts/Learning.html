Advice I've taken
Active Recall. I create questions before every lecture based on the slides given in class. I will make my notes after and during class based on the questions.
Spaced repetition. I schedule when I will review my questions, typically 3 days after a lecture
Practice problems from Cal Newport. I avoid rereading notes and almost always do every practice problem available over reading a textbook
Diffuse Mode vs Focus Mode from Barbara Oakley. If I'm stuck on a problem I will stop focusing on the problem to allow myself to ponder it in a diffuse state
