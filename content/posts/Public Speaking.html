Advice I've taken
Join Toastmasters - No matter how much advice I receive, I am never able to implement it unless I practice it. Toastmasters is a way to do this, you even get tailored feedback every time you present!
Storytelling > facts - No one remembers that stat you put in your presentation, but they will remember your stories
Your First Impression Is Key - I aim to appear confident and collected as soon as the audience can see me, even before I start speaking.
